Universal Referral System

This was a joint hackathon project that I participated in, back in April 2023 while I was still a student at the Technical University of Mombasa.
The hackathon was organized by Health IT and USAID.
This system was aimed at streamlining the referral process by reducing the paperwork involved. Also, the system was able to compare various resources and man power in various facilities so as to enable the referral officer to know where to refer the patient based on the type and urgency of the referral.

Ace Pay | Powered by Mpesa

This is a payment platform I developed to help SMEs to process cashless transfers efficiently.
The platform is powered by Mpesa, using the Daraja API.
The platform allows businesses to integrate the API into their POS systems so as to get payments directly into their Mpesa accounts or Bank accounts via Mpesa Paybill or Till number.
This reduces the risk of pay-after-purchase method of payment and also ease the process of processing earnings of the business.
